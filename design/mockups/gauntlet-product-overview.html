THE INSIGHT

The best accountability partner isn't a coach or an app notification. It's a friend you're trying to beat.

WHAT GAUNTLET IS (AND ISN'T)

GAUNTLET IS

✓

A competition platform that pulls data from your existing tracker

✓

A ranked system that gives every workout a purpose

✓

A social layer built on squads, leaderboards, and shareable content

✓

Built for everyone—from daily walkers to competitive athletes

GAUNTLET ISN'T

✗

A fitness tracker (it connects to ones you already own)

✗

A wellness or coaching app (no workout plans, no mindfulness)

✗

A health app (it competes on activity output, not body metrics)

✗

Apple-only or Strava-only (works across all major platforms)

COMPETITIONS

HEAD-TO-HEAD. REAL-TIME. ANY ACTIVITY.

Challenge a friend or group to a fitness competition. Pick the activity type (running, cycling, steps, etc.), choose the metric, set the timeframe. Real-time updates and push notifications keep everyone engaged.

The simplest unit of competition in Gauntlet—and the one that gets people hooked.

GAUNTLET

SQUADS & THE GAUNTLET

Team competition adds a layer of accountability that solo challenges can't match. You don't just let yourself down—you let your squad down.

SQUADS

2–10 friends form a squad. Compete as a unit against other squads of similar size and rank. Individual and group leaderboards within each squad.

THE GAUNTLET

Large-group matchups. Your 12 vs. their 12. One week. Total distance. Real-time progress tracking. Built for clubs, dorms, fraternities, friend groups, and teams.

SOCIAL PRESSURE

Push notifications when your squad's falling behind. Real-time updates when someone passes you. The group chat comes alive.

RIVALRY & IDENTITY

Squads build rivalries over time. Matchup history, head-to-head records, and victory badges create persistent competitive identity.

GAUNTLET

THE RANKED SYSTEM

7 TIERS. EVERY RANK EARNED.

Rank is calculated from three factors: activity volume, consistency, and competitive results. This ensures every rank is earned through sustained effort—not one big weekend. You compete within your tier, so every matchup feels winnable.

BRONZE BOTTOM 40%
SILVER 40–60%
GOLD 60–80%
PLATINUM 80–90%
DIAMOND 90–97%
TITANIUM 97–99%
OBSIDIAN TOP 1%

Inspired by ranked systems in competitive gaming. Each tier has subdivisions (e.g., Gold I, Gold II, Gold III). Rank creates aspirational identity—people describe themselves as a "Diamond player."

THE GAP IN THE MARKET

No existing product combines deep gamification, tiered ranked competition, squad mechanics, multi-platform integration, and shareable social content in a single app built for everyone.

Simple ring-closing. No squads, no ranks, no data viz, Apple-only.
